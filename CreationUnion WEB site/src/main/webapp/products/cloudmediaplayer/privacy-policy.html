Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 10, 2025

This policy explains how CloudMediaPlayer 2025 handles account information, cloud drive tokens, device data, advertising identifiers, and crash reports.

We collect only minimal data needed for playback, ads, and app stability.
We do not store your personal files, cloud passwords, or media on our own servers.
Your data lives locally on your device unless you choose to connect third-party services.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide the App's core functionality and monetization features.

Information you provide

  • Account information: If you choose to log in with a local account, the information is stored locally on your device.
  • Cloud drive credentials: When you link Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox, or Baidu Netdisk, the App receives an OAuth token and stores it securely on your device only.

Information collected automatically

  • Device information: Device model, operating system version, and Advertising ID may be collected for functionality and advertising.
  • Crash logs & performance data: Anonymous reports may be collected to improve stability and fix bugs.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide features: Play videos and display images from local storage or linked cloud drives.
  • To maintain settings: Save playback progress, preferences, and playlists locally.
  • For advertising: Display ads through Google AdMob.
  • For payments: Process VIP subscriptions and purchases through Google Play Billing.

4. Third-Party Services

We use third-party services that may collect information used to identify you. Please review the privacy policies of the services you use.

Important: CloudMediaPlayer acts as a client for these services. Your use of Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox, and Baidu Netdisk remains governed by their own privacy policies.

5. Data Safety & Retention

  • Local storage: Media thumbnails, cached files, and cloud drive tokens are stored locally on your device.
  • No file uploads: We do not upload your personal videos, photos, or file contents to any external server owned by us.
  • Deletion: Clearing the App's data or uninstalling the App removes local app data from your device.

6. Subscriptions & Payments

All financial transactions for VIP subscriptions and lifetime purchases are handled directly by Google Play. We do not collect or store your credit card information.

7. Children’s Privacy

These services do not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. You are advised to review this page periodically for any changes.
