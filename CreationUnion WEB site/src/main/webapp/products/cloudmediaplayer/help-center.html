1. Getting Started

  1. Download: Install CloudMediaPlayer from the Google Play Store.
  2. Sign in: Use your Google Account for full VIP features.
  3. Guest mode: Tap Skip to use the app without an account. VIP features and Google Play billing are unavailable in this mode.
Tip: If you want subscriptions, account sync, and multi-device VIP access, always sign in with the same Google account used for purchase.

2. Linking Cloud Drives

  1. Open Drive Management.
  2. Tap Link Drive and follow the authorization prompts.
  3. Choose the cloud account you want to connect and confirm the requested permissions.

Pro tip: VIP users can add multiple accounts from the same provider, such as two different Google Drive accounts.

3. Playback & Customization

Playback modes

  • Sequential: Play media in order.
  • Random: Shuffle the playback order.
  • Favorites First: Prioritize items you liked.

Display settings

  • Fit: Show the entire image, even if black bars appear.
  • Fill: Expand to cover the full screen and crop from the center.
  • Schedule: Set sleep/wake timers, such as auto-play from 8 AM to 10 PM.

4. Interface Breakdown

Main navigation

  • Account: Profile, VIP status, and billing.
  • System Settings: Cache and file filters.
  • Drive Management: Add or reorder cloud sources.
  • Playback Settings: Slideshow and display preferences.

Cache & storage

  • Cache limit: Set a maximum storage cap, such as 512 MB, to prevent the app from filling up your device.
  • Sync on Wi-Fi only: Keep this enabled to avoid unexpected cellular data charges.

5. Full-Screen Player

  • Interactive controls: Tap once to show the control bar. You can Like, Dislike, Pause, or Skip.
  • Auto-hide: The UI automatically vanishes after a few seconds of inactivity for an immersive viewing experience.

6. Troubleshooting FAQ

  • Why isn't my VIP status showing up? Make sure you are logged in to the same Google Play account used for the purchase.
  • Why are some files missing from the app? Check your Ignore Files settings in System Settings. The app may also hide files that exceed your size limits or use formats your older device cannot decode.
  • Why does the image quality seem lower than the original? On legacy devices, the app automatically optimizes image resolution to keep the slideshow smooth and stable.

7. Support & Privacy

Data safety: We do not read or collect your documents. Only media files in selected directories are indexed.
