CloudMediaPlayer User Guide
Overview
CloudMediaPlayer is an all-in-one media player for Android designed to aggregate your favorite cloud storage services. Seamlessly stream and manage content from Google Drive, OneDrive, Dropbox, and Baidu Netdisk in one place.
Breathe New Life into Old Devices: Optimized for Android 7.0+. Turn your retired tablets or phones into dedicated high-tech photo frames.
The Ultimate Family Hub: Perfect for families to share memories. Multiple contributors can upload to different cloud accounts, while parents enjoy a single, unified slideshow of the whole family.

Key Features
Cloud Aggregation: VIP members can link multiple accounts from various providers. Free users enjoy one cloud connection at no cost.
Offline Ready: Download and sync cloud media to local cache. Enjoy smooth playback while saving your data and bandwidth.
Flexible VIP Plans: Manage subscriptions via Google Play. One VIP account covers up to 3 devices.
Smart Hardware Detection: Automatically filters out file types that your specific device hardware cannot handle, ensuring a crash-free experience.
Privacy First: We only access media files in folders you designate. Your documents and personal files remain strictly private.

Getting Started
1. Setup
Download CloudMediaPlayer from the Google Play Store.
Sign In: Use your Google Account for full VIP features.
Guest Mode: Tap "Skip" to use the app without an account (Note: VIP features and Google Play billing are unavailable in this mode).
2. Linking Cloud Drives
Navigate to "Drive Management."
Tap "Link Drive" and follow the prompts to authorize your accounts.
Pro Tip: VIP users can add multiple accounts of the same service (e.g., two different Google Drive accounts).
3. Playback & Customization
Browse your cloud folders in the "Playback" tab.
Modes: Choose between Sequential, Random, or Favorites First.
Display Settings:
Fit: Shows the entire image (may leave black bars).
Fill: Expands the image to cover the full screen (crops from the center).
Schedule: Set a "Sleep/Wake" timer (e.g., Auto-play from 8 AM to 10 PM).

Interface Breakdown
Main Navigation
The bottom navigation bar provides quick access to:
Account: Profile, VIP status, and Billing.
System Settings: Cache and file filters.
Drive Management: Add or reorder your cloud sources.
Playback Settings: Slideshow and display preferences.
Cache & Storage
Cache Limit: Set a maximum storage cap (e.g., 512MB) to prevent the app from filling up your device.
Sync on Wi-Fi Only: Keep this toggled ON to avoid unexpected cellular data charges.
The Full-Screen Player
Interactive Controls: Tap once to show the control bar. You can Like (👍), Dislike (👎), Pause, or Skip.
Auto-Hide: The UI will automatically vanish after a few seconds of inactivity for an immersive viewing experience.

Troubleshooting (FAQ)
Q: Why isn't my VIP status showing up? A: Ensure you are logged into the same Google Play account used for the purchase.
Q: Why are some files missing from the app? A: Check your "Ignore Files" settings in System Settings. The app may also hide files that exceed your size limits or use formats your older device cannot decode.
Q: Why does the image quality seem lower than the original? A: On legacy devices, the app automatically optimizes image resolution to ensure the slideshow runs smoothly without lagging or crashing.

Support & Privacy
Data Safety: We do not read or collect your documents. Only media files in selected directories are indexed.
